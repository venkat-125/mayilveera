Exploring the Role of Mayilveera Polymer

Mayilveera Polymer plays a crucial role in delivering high-quality woven solutions used across various sectors. Our products serve a wide range of applications — from packaging and storage to industrial-grade uses — reflecting our commitment to versatility, durability, and customer satisfaction.

Tape Plant Machine

A high-performance system used to produce polypropylene tapes, which are the base material for manufacturing woven sacks and bags.

Circular Looms

High-speed machines that weave polypropylene tapes into continuous circular fabric, ideal for manufacturing woven bags and sacks.

Lamination Machine

A machine used to coat woven fabric with a protective polypropylene layer, enhancing strength, durability, and moisture resistance.

Bag Conversion Machine

A machine that converts laminated or unlaminated woven fabric into finished bags through cutting, stitching, and other finishing processes.
